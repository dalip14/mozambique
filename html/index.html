BACK TO THE FUTURE

The Andre cabinet draws on historical references as diverse as the tactile surfaces of Jazz Age interiors to mid-century Brutalism. The glow of the in-house cast brass handle contrasts with the warm texture of Barkskin clad doors; together they exude a quiet luxury. The top and base in glossy black frames the cabinet to make for a compositionally striking piece that is historical yet futuristic.
